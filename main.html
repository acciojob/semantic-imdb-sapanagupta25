Movie Summary

"Inception" is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of extracting valuable secrets from people's subconscious through shared dreams. When he is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven, Cobb must do the impossible—implant an idea into someone's mind. With a team of experts, he dives into the dream world, facing unexpected challenges that blur the line between reality and illusion.
